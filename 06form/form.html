New Season

Shetland season 8 sees Ashley Jensen taking over detecting duties from the previous lead, Douglas Henshall. Douglas, who has been with the series since the very beginning as DI Jimmy Perez, said goodbye to the series in season 7. Thankfully, that’s not the end of the show though, with Extras star Ashley coming into the hugely popular BBC crime drama. Ashley is teaming up on a case with Shetland regular Alison O’Donnell as Detective Inspector "Tosh" McIntosh.
